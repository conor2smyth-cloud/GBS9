Tonight’s Menu

Printing Instructions:
- On desktop: Press Ctrl + P (Windows/Linux) or Cmd + P (Mac).
- In the print dialog, select your printer or “Save as PDF”.
- Recommended sizes: A4 Portrait for standard menus, or A5 Portrait for smaller menus.
- You can adjust the size in the “More settings → Paper size” dropdown.
- Borders and buttons will be hidden automatically.
