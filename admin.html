Q: How do I add cocktails to tonight’s event?

A: Sign in, open a tab, tick the drinks you want, then click Save Tonight’s Menu. Guests will see updates instantly.

Q: How do I print tonight’s menu?

A: Click 🖨️ Print Menu. This opens a printable version in a new tab. Use Ctrl+P or Cmd+P.

Q: Can I hide a drink later?

A: Yes. Just untick it in the selector and save again. It disappears from the guest menu immediately.
