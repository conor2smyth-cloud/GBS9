Q: How do I add cocktails to tonight’s event?

A: Sign in, open the tabs, tick the drinks you want. Click Save. Guests will see them instantly under Tonight’s Event > Sip.

Q: How do I print tonight’s menu?

A: Click Print Menu. This opens a printable view. Use Ctrl+P or Cmd+P.

Q: Can I hide a drink later?

A: Yes. Untick it and click Save. Guests will no longer see it.
